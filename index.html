BibleDaily Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Introduction

BibleDaily ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our BibleDaily mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

BibleDaily is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal personal information:

  • SOAP Journal Entries: Your personal reflections, observations, applications, and prayers that you choose to write in the app
  • Reading Progress: Your daily Bible reading completion status and progress through the yearly reading plan
  • App Preferences: Your language settings (English/Chinese), notification preferences, and Bible translation selections

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app functionality
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance (no personal data)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the BibleDaily app functionality
  • Save and sync your SOAP journal entries across devices
  • Track your Bible reading progress
  • Send daily reading reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when needed

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on your device and, if you choose to sync, in secure cloud storage. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

BibleDaily may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Bible Gateway: For accessing Bible passages (opens in external browser)
  • Word Project: For audio Bible features (opens in external browser)
  • Share Services: For sharing SOAP entries via WhatsApp or other apps (uses system sharing)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Modify or delete your SOAP journal entries
  • Change your notification preferences
  • Switch between English and Chinese language settings
  • Disable data collection features

Children's Privacy

BibleDaily is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@bibledaily.app
  • Website: https://bibledaily.app/privacy

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

International Users

If you are accessing BibleDaily from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of September 20, 2025, and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page.
