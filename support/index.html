Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start a reading plan?

A: Open the app and navigate to the Home screen. The daily reading plan will automatically show today's passages. Simply tap on a passage to read it.

Q: How does verse memorization work?

A: Go to the Memorize tab and add verses you want to memorize. The app will create a 5-week plan with daily practice and review schedules.

Q: Can I share verses with friends?

A: Yes! You can export verses as text, JSON files, or QR codes to share with friends who also use BibleDaily.

Q: How do I change Bible translations?

A: Go to Settings and select your preferred translation (NIV, ESV, KJV, NKJV, etc.).
